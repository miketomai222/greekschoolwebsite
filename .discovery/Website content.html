Headline

Connecting Generations Through Greek Language & Culture

Subheadline

A welcoming school for children and adults to learn Greek, explore history, and engage with Greek traditions

For Children (In-person classes every Saturday)


Our Saturday school is designed for children to develop their Greek language skills naturally while immersing themselves in culture, history, and traditions. Led by dedicated teachers who are passionate about both education and heritage, our classes help children grow their confidence in speaking, reading, and writing Greek while staying connected to their roots.


We offer classes from Reception to A Levels, ensuring that every child can learn at their own pace and progress in a structured way.


What to expect:

  • Engaging lessons that develop speaking, reading, and writing skills, including participation in a student newspaper.

  • Greek customs and celebrations, from traditional dances and parades to theatrical sketches.

  • A love for Greek literature, with weekly book borrowing and guest readings by Greek authors.

  • A warm and supportive environment where children feel part of a community.




For Adults (In-person on Wednesdays & Online on Mondays)

Whether you're reconnecting with your Greek or Cypriot roots or preparing for your next trip to Greece & Cyprus, our adult classes focus on practical language skills in a welcoming and engaging environment. Led by experienced educators, our lessons help you speak, read, and understand Greek naturally, with the flexibility to learn in person or online.

Tuition Fees & Payment Information


For Children (Reception to A Levels)


As a non-profit charity, our fees are set every half term based on the number of students enrolled. This ensures we can cover rent, teacher salaries, and essential operational costs while keeping tuition as low as possible.


For children, fees are paid per term to ensure the sustainability of our school. We also offer sibling discounts to support families with more than one child attending.


For Adults (In-Person & Online Classes)


Adult learners follow a separate fee structure and pay on a monthly basis.
