Our Story

Our school began with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide children with a place to learn Greek, embrace their heritage, and connect with Greek culture. What started as a small gathering of just nine children has grown into a thriving educational community.

Today, we welcome over 50 students and operate from the state-of-the-art facilities at St. Mary's Catholic School. While our school has grown, our heart remains the same: to pass down the language, traditions, and history that connect us to Greece and to each other.

Whether you're a parent looking to strengthen your child's connection to their heritage or an adult ready to start your journey in Greek, you'll find a welcoming community here.
